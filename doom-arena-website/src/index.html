How the Gameplay is verified onchain

STEP 01

Play the run

Your keystrokes hit the riv engine running the game on Linux in a Cartesi Machine on the Browser. After the session, riv generates a deterministic gameplay log.

STEP 02

Submit to InputBox

The gameplay is posted as an input to the Cartesi Rollups InputBox onchain.

STEP 03

Node processes

The Cartesi node executes your gameplay inside the Cartesi Machine. It will generate the same result as everything is deterministic.

STEP 04

Epoch closes

The node submits an epoch claim onchain with the root-hash of its inputs. Once the epoch is accepted, your score is set, anyone can verify it onchain.
